Map of Andøya, Norway

My heritage starts with my maternal grandparents, who were born and grew up on the island of Andøya, Norway.

Andøya is the northernmost island in the Vesterålen archipelago (69 deg N), situated about 300 kilometres (190 miles) north of the Arctic circle. Andøya, is the 10th largest island in Norway, and is located in the Nordland county. The island is sparsly populated with only ~ 5,000 residents. Andenes is the only town on the island with a population of 2,700. Andenes is one of the largest finsihing ports in Norway.
